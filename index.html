About Me

Hi, I'm Pyx, a freelance video editor specializing in YouTube vlogs and short-form content for creators. I love turning messy timelines into tight, watchable stories that keep audiences engaged from hook to outro.

My process focuses on pacing, narrative clarity, captions, and tasteful sound design-always tailored to the platform and your brand voice. Whether you're scaling shorts or polishing full-length vlogs, I'll help your content punch above its weight.
